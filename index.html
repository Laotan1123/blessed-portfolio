Our team of experienced dieticians and nutritionists works closely with clients to develop customized diet meal plans tailored to their specific goals, dietary preferences, and health conditions. Whether it's weight management, athletic performance, or managing a medical condition, our meal plans are designed to provide balanced and delicious options.

Catering Services

Our catering services extend beyond regular events and gatherings. We cater specialized diet meals for various institutions, corporate offices, and healthcare facilities. Our culinary experts ensure that each meal meets the highest standards of nutrition and taste. Whether it's accommodating specific dietary requirements or catering to a large group, ILD Nutrition Consultations delivers excellence in every aspect.

Maternity Nutrition Program

ILD Nutrition Consultations takes pride in its long-standing collaboration with hospitals, particularly the Maternity department. Expecting and nursing mothers undergo significant physiological changes that demand proper nourishment for their health and the optimal development of their babies. Our specialized Maternity Nutrition Program provides essential information and personalized meal plans to support these mothers throughout their pregnancy and postpartum journey. By offering guidance on essential nutrients, portion control, and dietary modifications, we strive to contribute to the well-being of both mother and child.

Packed Meal Plans:

Recognizing the modern lifestyle's demands, ILD Nutrition Consultations has devised convenient packed meal plans for busy students, professionals, and workers. These pre-prepared meals are nutritionally balanced and thoughtfully portioned, enabling individuals to maintain a healthy diet even on-the-go. Our packed meal plans take the guesswork out of healthy eating, ensuring that our clients can focus on their studies, work, or other responsibilities with sustained energy and vitality.

Proposal: Empowering Zimbabweans in Nigeria NGO

Introduction:


In the heart of Nigeria, a community of Zimbabweans faces numerous challenges as they strive to integrate and prosper in their adopted country. Recognizing the pressing need to support and uplift our compatriots, we propose the establishment of an NGO dedicated to empowering Zimbabweans in Nigeria. This organisation will focus on addressing the multifaceted challenges encountered by Zimbabweans residing in Nigeria, with a primary emphasis on fostering self-sustainability and financial independence.Our comprehensive approach aligns with Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) 1, 8, 10, and 17, ensuring impactful outcomes for both our beneficiaries and our partners.

Mission Statement:


Our mission is to empower Zimbabweans in Nigeria by providing support, resources, and opportunities that enable them to overcome challenges, achieve self-sustainability, and thrive in their chosen endeavours,contributing to the following SDGs:

SDG 1: No Poverty
SDG 8: Decent Work and Economic Growth
SDG 10: Reduced Inequality

Objectives:



Financial Empowerment: We aim to equip Zimbabweans with the tools, knowledge, and resources necessary to start and sustain businesses. Through training, mentorship, and access to funding opportunities, contributing to SDG 1 and SDG 8.

Employment Assistance: We will establish a network to connect Zimbabwean job seekers with employment opportunities in Nigeria. By collaborating with local businesses and organisations, we aim to facilitate job placements and career development initiatives tailored to the needs of our community members, in support of SDG 8.

Counselling and Support Services: Recognizing the importance of mental and emotional well-being, we will provide counselling and support services to assist Zimbabweans in navigating the challenges of living abroad. Through group sessions, individual counselling, and peer support networks, we aim to foster resilience, positivity, and a sense of belonging within our community, contributing to SDG 3: Good Health and Well-being.

Community Engagement: We will actively engage Zimbabweans in Nigeria through community events, workshops, and outreach programs. By creating a platform for dialogue, collaboration, and collective action, we seek to harness the collective strength and talents of our community to address shared challenges and pursue common goals, in alignment with SDG 10: Reduced Inequality.

Fundraising and Sustainability: To support our initiatives, we will engage in fundraising efforts aimed at securing financial support from individuals, businesses, and philanthropic organisations. Additionally, we will explore opportunities for generating revenue through income-generating projects and partnerships with local stakeholders,in alignment with SDG 17: Partnerships for the Goals.

Collaboration and Partnerships: We recognize the importance of collaboration and partnership in achieving our objectives. Conclusion:

The establishment of an NGO dedicated to empowering Zimbabweans in Nigeria represents a crucial step towards addressing the challenges faced by our community and building a brighter future for all. By working together with like-minded individuals and organisations, we can create a supportive and enabling environment where every member has the opportunity to thrive and succeed.
